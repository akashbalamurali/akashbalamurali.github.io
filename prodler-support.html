About Prodler

Prodler is a versatile productivity tool designed to help users efficiently manage their time and activities. With Prodler, users can easily track various activities they engage in throughout the day and analyze their time usage patterns. Whether you're a busy professional, a student managing multiple projects, or simply someone looking to improve time management skills, Prodler offers intuitive features to streamline your productivity.

Key Features

  • Activity Tracking: Users can log their activities, including work tasks, study sessions, exercise routines, leisure activities, and more. Prodler allows users to input the duration and timestamp of each activity, providing a comprehensive overview of how they spend their time.
  • Data Visualization: Prodler offers powerful charting capabilities, enabling users to visualize their activity data in various formats such as pie charts and bar graphs. Users can gain insights into their time distribution across different categories and identify trends in their activity patterns over time.
  • Secure and Private: Prodler prioritizes user privacy and data security. All user data is stored locally on the device, ensuring confidentiality and peace of mind. The app does not collect any personal information or transmit data to external servers.
