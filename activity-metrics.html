Activity Metrics

Prodler provides detailed metrics and statistics to help you understand your productivity habits better.

Available Metrics

  • Daily Statistics: View time spent on each activity per day
  • Weekly Reports: Analyze your activity patterns over the week
  • Monthly Trends: Track long-term productivity patterns
  • Category Analysis: Compare time spent across different activity categories

Understanding Your Metrics

Access your activity metrics through the Statistics tab to gain insights into your time management habits and make informed decisions about your daily routines.
